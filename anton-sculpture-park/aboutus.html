Park History

Anton Smit Sculpture Park was established to exhibit large-scale sculptures and provide a tranquil outdoor space for visitors to experience art in nature. Over the years the park has grown into a regional attraction hosting exhibitions, workshops and private events.

About Anton Smit

Anton Smit is a South African sculptor known for his monumental steel works that reflect the shapes and movement of the sea and coastline. His work blends industrial materials with natural settings.

Mission & Values

  • Bring public art to outdoor spaces
  • Encourage education and appreciation of sculpture
  • Host cultural events and community workshops
